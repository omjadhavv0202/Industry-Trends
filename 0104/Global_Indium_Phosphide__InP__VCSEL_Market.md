<h1>Global Indium Phosphide (InP) VCSEL Market Research Report 2025(Status and Outlook)</h1><br/><p>Indium Phosphide (InP) Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser (VCSEL) is a semiconductor-based laser diode that emits light vertically from its surface, differing from Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) and Light Emitting Diodes (LED). InP VCSELs are widely used for long-wavelength applications, particularly in telecommunications, data centers, sensing applications, and medical devices. These lasers offer advantages such as high efficiency, compact size, and superior modulation bandwidth, making them a preferred choice in various industries.</p>
