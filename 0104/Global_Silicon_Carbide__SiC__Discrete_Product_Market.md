 <p><strong>Advancements in 5G Infrastructure:</strong> The deployment of 5G networks requires high-frequency and high-power components, positioning SiC as a preferred material.</p>

 <p><strong>Limited Availability of Raw Materials:</strong> The supply chain for silicon carbide is not as established as for silicon, posing challenges for scalability.</p>
