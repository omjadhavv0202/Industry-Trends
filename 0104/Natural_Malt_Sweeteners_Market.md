 <p>Natural malt sweeteners, derived primarily from cereal grains like barley, wheat, and corn, are gaining popularity as clean-label and non-GMO alternatives to synthetic sweeteners. They are rich in maltose, a naturally occurring sugar, and are extensively used in the food and beverage industry. These sweeteners come in both liquid and powdered forms, catering to diverse applications such as bakery, beverages, and processed foods.</p>
