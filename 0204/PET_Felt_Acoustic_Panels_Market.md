 <p>PET felt acoustic panels are sound-absorbing panels made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET), a type of plastic often sourced from recycled materials, such as plastic bottles. These panels are designed to improve indoor acoustics by reducing noise and reverberation in spaces like offices, homes, and commercial environments. PET felt acoustic panels are lightweight, durable, and available in a wide range of colors, shapes, and textures, making them an eco-friendly and versatile solution for both functional sound management and aesthetic interior design.</p>
