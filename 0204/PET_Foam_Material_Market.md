 <p>PET foam material is a lightweight, rigid foam made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET), a type of polyester. This foam is known for its excellent mechanical properties, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, making it an ideal core material in composite structures. It is widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, marine, and wind energy for applications like sandwich panels, insulation, and structural components. PET foam is also valued for being environmentally friendly, as it can be made from recycled PET bottles and is fully recyclable itself. Its combination of strength, durability, and sustainability makes PET foam a versatile and popular choice in various engineering and manufacturing fields.</p>
