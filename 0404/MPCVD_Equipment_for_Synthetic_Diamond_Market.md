 <p>MPCVD microwave plasma chemical vapor deposition technology is a technique that excites reaction gases into a plasma state through microwave excitation, increases the chemical reaction rate, reduces the reaction temperature, and can form a stable spherical plasma within a large vacuum range. It minimizes the changes in plasma state caused by fluctuations in gas flow, pressure, gas composition, voltage, and other factors, thereby ensuring the continuity of single crystal material growth, To provide strong assurance for the synthesis of high-quality large-sized single crystal diamonds. Suitable for preparing high-quality hard films and crystals with large area, good uniformity, high purity, and good crystal morphology. MPCVD technology is currently the most widely used method for preparing single crystal diamond both domestically and internationally. Due to its ability to prepare large-area, high-quality diamonds, high safety factor, and pollution-free preparation process, MPCVD technology is considered the most ideal method for preparing artificial diamonds in the future.</p>
