 <p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) equipment, also known as vacuum-assisted closure (VAC) therapy, is a medical technique and device-driven therapy used in the treatment of chronic or complex wounds. NPWT involves the application of controlled negative pressure to the wound environment through specialized equipment, aiming to promote wound healing, reduce edema, stimulate granulation tissue formation, and mitigate the risk of infection.</p>
