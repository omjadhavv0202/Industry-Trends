 <p>m-Phenylenediamine (mPDA) is also known as 1,3-phenylenediamine (1,3-Benzenediamine, 1,3-Phenylenediamine), 1,3-diaminobenzene (1,3-Diaminobenzene), referred to as MPD. It is a white needle-shaped crystal at room temperature, soluble in ethanol, water, chloroform, acetone, dimethylamide, slightly soluble in ether, carbon tetrachloride, and difficult to dissolve in benzene, toluene, butanol. It is unstable in the air and easily turns light red. This product is flammable, and the combustion decomposition products are carbon monoxide, carbon dioxide, and nitrogen oxides. Metaphenylenediamine is a toxic chemical that irritates the eyes and skin.</p>
