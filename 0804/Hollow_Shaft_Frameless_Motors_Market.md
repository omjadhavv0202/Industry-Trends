 <p>Hollow Frameless Motors, also called frameless motors or frameless brushless motors, are electric motors that have no external housing or frame. Instead, it consists of a rotor and stator with no surrounding structure. This design allows for a more compact and lightweight motor compared to conventional framed motors. Hollow frameless motors are highly customizable and can be directly integrated into a variety of systems, offering advantages such as reduced weight, increased efficiency, and greater design and application flexibility. They are commonly used in robotics, aerospace, medical equipment, industrial automation, and other applications where space, weight, and precision are critical factors.</p>
