 <p data-end="658" data-start="103"><span style="font-size:16px;">Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC) is a semi-synthetic polymer derived from cellulose, commonly used in the construction industry as a thickening agent, water-retaining agent, and binder in various cement-based products. As a versatile material, it plays a crucial role in improving the performance and durability of construction materials, including cementitious mortars, adhesives, plasters, and grout formulations. HPMC???s ability to retain water and enhance workability makes it an indispensable additive in a wide range of construction applications.</span></p>
