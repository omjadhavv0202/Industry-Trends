 <p data-end="733" data-start="88"><span style="font-size:16px;">The <strong data-end="114" data-start="92">Fracking Fluid End</strong> market pertains to the component of hydraulic fracturing systems used in oil and natural gas production. These fluid ends are critical to frac pumps, which are the driving force behind the hydraulic fracturing process. Hydraulic fracturing, or "fracking," involves injecting high-pressure fluid into underground rock formations to release oil or natural gas. The <strong data-end="491" data-start="478">fluid end</strong> of the frac pump is where the hydraulic fluid is introduced and pressurized before it is forced into the wellbore. This is typically done using a high-power pump system that uses the fluid end to transfer the pressurized fluid into the well.</span></p>
