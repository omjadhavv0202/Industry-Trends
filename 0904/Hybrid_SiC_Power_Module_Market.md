 <p data-end="1594" data-start="1003"><span style="font-size:16px;">A <strong data-end="1050" data-start="1005">Hybrid Silicon Carbide (SiC) Power Module</strong> combines the high performance of <strong data-end="1109" data-start="1084">Silicon Carbide (SiC)</strong> with <strong data-end="1124" data-start="1115">IGBTs</strong> and <strong data-end="1148" data-start="1129">Schottky diodes</strong>.
