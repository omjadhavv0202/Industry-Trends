 <p><span style="font-size:16px;"><strong>Liqueurs</strong>, often referred to as cordials or schnapps in some regions, are alcoholic beverages made by blending distilled spirits with flavorings and sweeteners. These flavorings commonly include fruits, nuts, herbs, spices, flowers, or cream. The alcohol content in liqueurs typically ranges from 15% to 30% ABV (alcohol by volume), although it may vary depending on the brand and variety.</span></p>
