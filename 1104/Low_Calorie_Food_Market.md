<h1>Low-Calorie Food Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Low-Calorie Food refers to dietary products with significantly reduced calorie content. These foods are specifically formulated to provide minimal energy intake while maintaining essential nutrients. Typically, a low-calorie diet consists of 800 kilocalories (3,300 kJ) per day or less, aimed at weight management and overall health improvement. The demand for low-calorie foods has surged due to increasing health awareness, rising obesity rates, and the growing preference for nutritious food choices. This category includes various food and beverage products such as sugar substitutes, diet beverages, low-fat dairy products, and calorie-controlled snacks.</p>
