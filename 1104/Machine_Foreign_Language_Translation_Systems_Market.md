There are mainly three types of machine translation methods: Rules-based machine translation, Statistical machine translation, Neural machine translation. Machine translation can work without human intervention to translate considerable amounts of information that cannot be translated conventionally. The apparent benefits of machine translation as following: saves time, reduces costs, and memorizes terms.
