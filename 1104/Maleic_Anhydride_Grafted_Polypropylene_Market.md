<h1>Maleic Anhydride Grafted Polypropylene Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Maleic anhydride grafted polypropylene (MAH-g-PP) is a type of polypropylene that has been chemically modified by grafting maleic anhydride onto its polymer backbone. This modification enhances the polypropylene&#39;s compatibility with other materials, such as fillers, reinforcements, and various polymers, improving its adhesion and bonding properties. MAH-g-PP is commonly used in applications where improved interfacial bonding is crucial, such as in automotive parts, construction materials, and packaging. Its ability to enhance the performance of composite materials and improve their mechanical and thermal properties makes it valuable in a wide range of industrial applications.</p>
