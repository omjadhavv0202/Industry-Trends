<h1>Non-alcoholic Malt Beverages Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Non-alcoholic malt beverages are brewed drinks made primarily from barley and malt, containing little to no alcohol content. They are produced through a controlled brewing process where the alcohol is removed or restricted, making them suitable for consumers who prefer non-alcoholic alternatives. These beverages include dairy-based malt drinks and carbonated malt sodas, offering various flavors and nutritional benefits. Popular across different demographics, non-alcoholic malt beverages cater to health-conscious consumers, individuals avoiding alcohol for religious reasons, and those seeking flavorful, refreshing drinks.</p>
