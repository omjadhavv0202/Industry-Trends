 <p data-end="640" data-start="82">The <strong data-end="105" data-start="86">Door Seal Strip</strong> market involves products designed to seal the gaps between doors and door frames to prevent air, water, dust, and noise from entering or escaping. These sealing strips are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings to enhance energy efficiency, improve comfort, and prevent external elements from affecting indoor environments. They are typically made from materials such as rubber, silicone, foam, and EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer), which offer durability, flexibility, and resistance to weathering.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>? </p>
 
 
 
 <p>? </p>
 
 
 
 <p data-end="1034" data-start="642">Door seal strips are essential components in energy conservation and insulation systems. By improving airtightness, they help reduce heating and cooling costs and enhance the overall environmental performance of buildings. These strips are also integral to noise reduction, making spaces more comfortable and less prone to the ingress of outdoor pollutants like dust, allergens, and moisture.</p>
