 <p>Impregnation Pitch refers to the pitch used to impregnate carbon products to reduce the porosity of the product, increase the bulk density or achieve the purpose of impermeability. Coal pitch is generally used for impregnation of carbon products, and petroleum pitch or synthetic resin is also used. It is widely used in isostatic pressing graphite, ultra-high power graphite electrode, nuclear graphite and other fields.</p>
