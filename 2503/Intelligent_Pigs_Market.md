<h1>Intelligent Pigs Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Intelligent pigs, also known as pipeline inspection gauges or pigs, are devices used in the oil and gas industry for the internal inspection and maintenance of pipelines. These devices are equipped with advanced sensors and instruments to collect data on various pipeline conditions, such as internal corrosion, wall thickness, cracks, and other potential defects. The collected data helps operators assess the integrity of the pipeline and plan necessary maintenance and repair activities to ensure safe and efficient operations. It involves using specialized devices called "pigs" inserted into pipelines and propelled through them using the flow of the transported product or by an external driving mechanism.</p>
