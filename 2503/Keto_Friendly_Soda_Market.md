<h1>Keto-Friendly Soda Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Keto-Friendly Soda refers to low-carbohydrate beverages designed for individuals following a ketogenic diet. These sodas typically contain little to no sugar, replacing traditional sweeteners with alternatives like stevia, erythritol, or monk fruit extract. Unlike conventional carbonated drinks, which are high in sugar and carbohydrates, keto-friendly sodas aim to provide a refreshing, flavorful option without disrupting ketosis.</p>
