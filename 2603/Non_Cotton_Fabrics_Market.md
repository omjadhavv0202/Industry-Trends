These fabrics are distinguished by the fact that they are made without the use of cotton, utilizing materials like silk, linen, ramie, wool, and leather, as well as various manmade fibers. This expansive group includes industrial fabrics such as fiberglass and carbon fabrics, specialty materials like flame-resistant fabric, and practical textiles such as laminated, stretch, or reflective fabric. The versatility and diversity of non-cotton fabrics make them indispensable in a variety of industries, including fashion, medical, military, sports, and transportation.</p>
