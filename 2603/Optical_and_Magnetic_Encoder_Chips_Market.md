 <p>Encoder chips are semiconductor components used to convert motion or position data into digital signals. Optical encoder chips use light-based sensors, while magnetic encoder chips rely on magnetic fields for signal detection. Both are essential in applications requiring precise motion control and position tracking.</p>

 <li><strong>Magnetic Encoders</strong>: Use magnetic fields for detection, providing durability in harsh environments and resistance to dust and debris.</li>
