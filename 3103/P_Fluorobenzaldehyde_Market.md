 <p>P-Fluorobenzaldehyde (CAS 459-57-4) is a colorless and transparent liquid with a molecular formula of C7H5FO. It is an important intermediate of medicines, dyes and pesticides. It is used in medicine to manufacture clofibrate aluminum salt, clofibrate, and p-fluorophenoxy isobutyric acid. It is used in dyes to prepare weak acid bright yellow 2G and weak acid bright yellow 5G. It is also used to prepare rice JFF, plant growth regulators, etc.</p>
