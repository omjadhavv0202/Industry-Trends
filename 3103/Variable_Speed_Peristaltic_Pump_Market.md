 <p>Peristaltic pumps, also known as roller pumps or hose pumps, are a type of positive displacement pump commonly used in the pharmaceutical industry for various applications. These pumps are characterized by their unique pumping mechanism, which involves squeezing a flexible tube or hose to create a series of moving "pockets" that move the fluid through the tubing. Peristaltic pumps are known for their gentle and precise fluid handling, making them suitable for pharmaceutical applications where accurate dosing, sterile transfer, and contamination-free pumping are essential.</p>
